Our Story So Far

Since 1992,GEM IMPORT & EXPORT has been a pioneer in brand-building and distribution of premium beauty and personal care products across Egypt. With over three decades of experience, we specialize in delivering innovative, high-quality haircare, skincare, personal care and professional cosmetic products that cater to diverse consumer needs. Our success is built on a carefully curated portfolio of global brands, strategic partnerships, and an extensive distribution network, allowing us to maintain a leading market presence while ensuring sustainable growth.
